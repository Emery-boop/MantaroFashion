Our Story

Luxury Meets
Street Culture

MANTARO Originals is an American-based luxury fashion brand founded in 2023 by Dr. Promise Chibueze Egbulefu. Built to bridge the gap between high-end fashion and streetwear, the brand creates premium sneakers, ready-to-wear clothing, accessories, and fragrances that embody both sophistication and cultural edge.

Headquartered in the United States, with operational bases in Doha, London, and Sydney, MANTARO Originals is positioned at the intersection of luxury and street culture — redefining modern fashion through innovation, identity, and conscious design. Every piece is rooted in intention, where storytelling meets craftsmanship.

Driven by a mission to challenge the status quo, MANTARO champions diversity, inclusivity, and representation, while actively fostering opportunities for emerging creative talent and entrepreneurship.
